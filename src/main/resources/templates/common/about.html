About Cafe Web App

Welcome to the Cafe Web App, your go-to solution for managing your cafe's operations seamlessly. Built using Spring Boot, this application offers a robust and scalable solution for cafe management.

Our app provides a range of features including order management, inventory tracking, customer management, and sales reporting. With an intuitive interface and powerful backend, managing your cafe has never been easier.

The Cafe Web App leverages the power of Spring Boot to deliver high performance and reliability. Whether you're a small cafe or a large chain, our app is designed to meet your needs and help you grow your business.
